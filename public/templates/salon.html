About LUXE Beauty

Founded in 2010, LUXE Beauty has been providing exceptional beauty services with a focus on quality, luxury, and personalized care. Our mission is to help clients look and feel their best in a relaxing, upscale environment.

All our technicians are licensed professionals who participate in ongoing education to stay current with the latest techniques and trends in the beauty industry.

We use only premium, cruelty-free products that deliver visible results while being gentle on your skin and hair.
